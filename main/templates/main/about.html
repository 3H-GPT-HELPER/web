Introducing our final year project: ChatGPT Helper!
ChatGPT Helper is an innovative project that focuses on organizing answers from ChatGPT within our own website, based on user preferences and utilizing an LDA (Latent Dirichlet Allocation) model. This approach allows us to provide users with a more tailored and efficient experience. By leveraging ChatGPT's conversational abilities and the LDA model's topic modeling capabilities, we can categorize and present answers in a structured manner that aligns with the user's chosen topics. This integration enhances the overall usability and navigability of our website, ensuring that users can easily access the information they need while engaging in meaningful conversations. With ChatGPT Helper, we strive to provide a unique and optimized chat experience that combines the power of AI-generated responses with intelligent organization through LDA modeling.
We value user feedback and encourage you to join us on this exciting journey with ChatGPT Helper! Your input is vital in helping us improve the system and create a more satisfying user experience. Whether it's suggestions, reporting issues, or sharing ideas for new features, your feedback plays a crucial role in shaping the future of ChatGPT Helper. By actively involving users like you, we can refine the AI capabilities, enhance the accuracy of responses, and ensure that ChatGPT Helper meets your needs effectively. Together, we can push the boundaries of AI-assisted conversations and create a tool that truly empowers users in various domains. Join us on this journey as we continue to evolve and improve ChatGPT Helper. Your feedback is invaluable, and we look forward to collaborating with you to make this chat platform even more remarkable. Let's embark on this exciting adventure together!
